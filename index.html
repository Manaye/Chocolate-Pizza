Chocolate Pizza

POSTED ON DEC 15 2013/DESSERTS

print

For the fig swirl:Melt butter over meduim heat in a saucepan.Add brown sugar and stir to disolve.Halve all of the figs and toss with water and lemon juice.cook over meduim heat,string frequently,until you have a chunky-jammy mixture.Add salt with one or two stirs,set aside and let cool completely.

Icecream:In a small pot over meduim heat,combine milk and granulated sugar until sugar is completely dissolved and the milk is barely lukewarm.Whisk in the egg yolks.set the mixture in the fridge and wait until the mixture is cooled.

using an icecream machine,pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacurer instruction, i.e, lets spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware.Freeze for at least two hours before serving.

1 1/2 milk
1/2 cup mascarpone
1/2 tsp pink salt
1 lb Black Mission Figs
1/2 cup brown sugar
2-4 tbsp water
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
1/3 granulated suger
2 egg yolks
1 lemon,juiced
tbsp butter
1 cup honey roastedpecans,roughly chopped
